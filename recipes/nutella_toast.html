Nutella Toast

Home

Nutella toast is a quick and easy recipe that can be made by even the worst of cooks. If you are someone who burns their boiled eggs, this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • Nutella
  • Sliced bread

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread.
  2. Spread a respectable amount of nutella onto each slice of toast.
  3. Enjoy!!!
