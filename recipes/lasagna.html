Lasagna

Home

Lasagna is an easy and delicious recipe that features pasta, tomato sauce, ground beef, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef.
  2. Place a layer of Lasagna noodles into the pan
  3. Place a layer of ground beef into the pan.
  4. Place a layer of tomato sauce into the pan.
  5. Place a layer of of cheese into the pan.
  6. Create 2 more layers.
  7. Top it off with one more layer of Lasagna noodles and one more layer of cheese.
  8. Place the whole thing in the oven at 350F for 30 mintes.
